Everything you need to know before making the switch to SmartHosp+.

We have a battle-tested data migration process with zero data loss guarantee. Our team extracts data from your existing system (regardless of format — Excel, legacy HMS, or paper records), validates and maps it to SmartHosp+ schema, and imports it with a full audit trail. You get to verify the migrated data before going live.

All plans include email and chat support. Hospital and Enterprise plans include phone support with response SLAs. Enterprise customers get a dedicated Customer Success Manager who conducts monthly reviews, proactively identifies optimization opportunities, and ensures you're getting maximum value from the platform.

We offer a 30-day pilot program rather than a generic free trial — because healthcare software needs to be tested in your actual environment with your actual workflows to be meaningful. During the pilot, we set up a fully configured sandbox environment with your department structure and run parallel operations so you can evaluate with zero risk.

SmartHosp+'s AI models are trained on anonymized data from our hospital network (with full consent) and continuously fine-tuned on your hospital's own patterns. The models predict patient inflow, flag patients at risk of readmission, detect billing anomalies, and surface inventory gaps — all in real-time on your dashboard. No ML expertise required from your end.

SmartHosp+ has an offline-first mode for critical modules including patient registration, vitals recording, and prescription writing. Data syncs automatically when the connection is restored. For hospitals in low-connectivity areas, we also offer a hybrid on-premise deployment option.
